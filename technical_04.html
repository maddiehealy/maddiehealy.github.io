Siamese cats are known for their slender bodies, blue almond-shaped eyes, and large ears. They are also quite vocal and love to interact with their owners.

Bengal cats are famous for their striking leopard-like appearance and energetic nature. They are very playful and love to climb and explore.

Persian cats are known for their long, luxurious fur and flat faces. They are calm, affectionate, and make great indoor companions.
